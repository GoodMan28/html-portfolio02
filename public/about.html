About Me

Shanti aur sukh ka anubhav kare, yeh rahe kahani ek bharatiya jeewan ki, jahaan ghee, chaai, aur namaste ka sangam hota hai. Ganga ke tat par baith kar sochta hoon, kya hai jeevan ka asli arth? Masala, rasam aur samosa ka swaad, yeh hai humari sanskriti ki mithaas aur teekhaapan. Holi ke rang, aur Diwali ki roshni, yeh hi toh hai humare apne desh ki chamak. Ghar ka khana, aur dosti ke anmol rishton ke saath bante hai humare apne yaadein. Bharatiya sanskriti ka rang biranga swaroop, vishwa bhar mein ek anokha sandesh hai. Aakhir mein, vasudhaiva kutumbakam, yah soch hai jo humein ek saath baandhti hai, ek anant parivaar mein.

Desh ke kone kone mein, alag alag rang aur boli, alag alag log aur unka jeevan. Vaha Rajasthan ke registaan mein ghoomti oont ki sawaari, aur Kashmir ke shant saari ke neeche shayari. Idhar Chennai ke nariyal ke ped aur udhar Punjab ke sarson ke khet. Yeh sab, humare apne Hindustan ka hissa hain. Baithkar kisi chhat par, chandni raat mein kahin se aati shehnai ki awaaz, aur mandir ke ghanton ki goonj se dil mein ek shanti ka ehsaas hota hai.

Vande Mataram ki awaaz jab subah ke waqt sunayi deti hai, toh man mein ek garv ka anubhav hota hai. Desh ki suraksha mein lage jawan, aur gaav gaav mein baste kisaan, yeh dono hi Bharat ke asli shaktistrot hain. Aur jab hum subah ki kadak adrak wali chai le kar sooraj ko ugte hue dekhte hain, toh ek naye din ka svagat karte hain. Thandi hawaon mein lehrate gehu ke kheton ki khushboo, aur barish ke pehle ki mitti ki mehak se hi toh bharatiya jeewan ki shuruaat hoti hai.

Humari parampara aur hamare tyohar, yeh sirf utsav nahi hain, yeh jeene ka tareeka hain. Ganesh Chaturthi par Ganpati Bappa ka aana, aur Navratri mein dandiya ki dhun par nachna, yeh hi toh hai Bharat ki asli pehchaan. Ek dusre ke saath mil jul kar, khushiyon ko baantna aur manate jana humare apne rishte aur sanskaar hain. Pongal, Baisakhi, Onam, aur Bihu, har rajya ki apni alag pehchaan, par hum sab ko ek rang mein rangta hai.
